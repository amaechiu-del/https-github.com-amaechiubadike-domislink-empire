# DomisLink Empire - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
DomisLink Empire is a comprehensive multi-platform ecosystem built on "ONE CODEBASE. ONE DATABASE. WORLDWIDE. AI-POWERED." philosophy. It provides integrated services across real estate, travel booking, flight monitoring, education, and administration through a unified monorepo architecture.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Unified Experience**: Single login, shared wallet, and consistent UI across all platforms
- **AI-Powered Intelligence**: 30+ AI characters for education, community moderation, and automated content management
- **Global Reach**: Multi-language support (20+ languages), geolocation intelligence, and worldwide community structure
- **Real-Time Integration**: Live flight data, real estate listings, and dynamic pricing across all services

## Key Applications

### 🏠 Real Estate Platform (realestate.domislink.com)
- Worldwide community-based property listings with hierarchical structure (Country → State → City → Community)
- AI-powered community creation and user engagement
- Automated listing lifecycle management (auto-archive after 2 hours, permanent deletion after 14 days)
- Service provider directory organized by community
- AI-moderated community discussion boards

### ✈️ TicketMaster (tickets.domislink.com)
- Live flight booking with real-time fare display
- Integration with Kiwi.com for authentic flight data
- Commission-based revenue model (2% hidden from public)
- International and national airline partnerships

### 📡 Flight Monitor (fm.domislink.com)
- Real-time worldwide flight tracking
- Airline-specific community forums for discussions and controversies
- Direct integration with TicketMaster for seamless booking transitions

### 🎓 TeachMaster (teachmaster.domislink.com)
- Gamified education platform with 30 exaggerated AI characters
- West African curriculum focus (JSS1-SS3, WAEC, NECO, JAMB)
- 30+ gamification mechanics including XP, streaks, leaderboards, and badges
- Spaced repetition flash cards and team-based study squads
- Character progression system with premium unlocks

### 🤖 Admin AI Builder (admin.domislink.com)
- Dedicated AI workspace for ecosystem development and maintenance
- Trained specifically on DomisLink architecture and patterns
- Obedient AI assistant for rapid feature development and system updates

### 🌐 Hub Landing Page (domislink.com)
- Central entry point showcasing all platform capabilities
- Unified branding and navigation to all sub-applications

## Target Users

- **Travel Enthusiasts**: Users booking flights and tracking aviation activities
