Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

🔧 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Examples:

  • domislink-session: Maintains your login session
  • domislink-theme: Remembers your theme preference
  • domislink-cookie-consent: Stores your cookie preferences
  • csrf-token: Security protection against attacks

Duration: Session or 1 year
Purpose: Site functionality and security

📊 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve user experience.

Examples:

  • _ga: Google Analytics visitor identification
  • _gid: Google Analytics session identification
  • domislink-analytics: Internal usage tracking

Duration: 2 years maximum
Purpose: Website analytics and improvement

🎯 Marketing Cookies (Optional)

Used to deliver personalized advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

Examples:

  • _fbp: Facebook Pixel tracking
  • ads-preferences: Advertisement personalization
  • campaign-tracking: Marketing campaign attribution

Duration: 90 days to 2 years
Purpose: Personalized advertising

⚙️ Preference Cookies (Optional)

Remember your settings and preferences to enhance your experience.

Examples:

  • language-preference: Your selected language
  • currency-preference: Your preferred currency
  • search-filters: Saved search preferences
  • dashboard-layout: Customized dashboard settings

Duration: 1 year
Purpose: Personalized user experience

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

🔧 Essential Cookies Disabled

  • Website may not function properly
  • Unable to maintain login sessions
  • Security features may be compromised
  • Theme preferences won't be saved

📊 Analytics Cookies Disabled

  • We can't improve user experience
  • No impact on site functionality
  • Less personalized recommendations

🎯 Marketing Cookies Disabled

  • Less relevant advertisements
  • No impact on site functionality
  • May see more generic ads

⚙️ Preference Cookies Disabled

  • Settings reset on each visit
  • Need to re-enter preferences
  • Less personalized experience

6. Cookie Retention Periods

Cookie Type Retention Period Purpose
Session Cookies Until browser closes Temporary functionality
Essential Cookies 1 year maximum Core site functions
Analytics Cookies 26 months maximum Usage analysis
Marketing Cookies 90 days to 2 years Advertisement targeting
Preference Cookies 1 year User preferences
