# TeachMaster - Introduction & Compliance Clarifications

## 🎮 GAMIFIED LEARNING PLATFORM

### **What is TeachMaster?**
TeachMaster is a **gamified educational platform** featuring 50 exaggerated AI tutors who make learning fun through:
- **Interactive gameplay mechanics** (XP, levels, badges, streaks)
- **Memorable character personalities** (funny, dramatic, eccentric)
- **Competitive elements** (leaderboards, study squads, challenges)
